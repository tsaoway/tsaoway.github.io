Hui Cao received his Ph.D. degree in Electronic Engineering at City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, in 2017. He is now an associate professor at School of Information and Engineering, Wuhan University of Technology, Wuhan, China.

His research interests include array signal processing, compressive sensing, TDOA estimation, multidimensional parameter estimation and so on.
